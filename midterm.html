Used Book Sale

Sponsored by the Friends of the Palo Alto Public Library

We have over 50,000 books, videos, records, and magazines at our monthly Saturday sale. Items are donated by individuals, estates, and businesses, and some materials are items that have been weeded from the Palo Alto Library's shelves to make room for new materials.

Come visit our friendly volunteers and pick up some new books at a deep discount. We donate 100% of the Book Sale proceeds to helping local libraries.

Prices

  • Hardcover books starting at $1
  • Paperback books starting at 50 cents
  • Bargain books: all hardcovers $1, all paperbacks 50 cents, all children's books 25 cents
  • All books in the bargain room are half off on Sundays!
  • Or buy a grocery bag for $5 and fill it with as many books as you can fit
  • Gift certificates available for purchase in $5 increments

Hours and Location

Adult materials are located in the Main Room (along the north driveway, adjacent to Charleston Shopping Center). Children's materials are located in the library's new room adjacent to Cubberley track, the pavillion, and the Greendell School). Bargain books are located in rooms K6 and K7.

The Main Room is open from 8 AM to 3 PM. Currently, only 160 customers at a time are allowed in the Main Room. Although not required, early arrivals may get a ticket to receive a place in line. Tickets are handed out between 8 AM and 11 AM.

The children's and bargain rooms are open from 7 AM to 3 PM.
